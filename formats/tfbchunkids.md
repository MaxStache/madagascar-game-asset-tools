# TFB inhouse RW Chunk IDs

TFB VENDOR ID: 0x800000

## 0x800000D4 (0xD4)

extension of a atomic sector

### Structure

Unknown, in tested always `03000f0f0000000000000000`

## 0x800000FE (0xFE)

extension of a atomic sector

### Structure

Example in Atomic Sector with geometry

`03000f0f000000000100000000000000`

Example in Atomic Sector with NO geometry

`03000f0f000000000000000000000000`
